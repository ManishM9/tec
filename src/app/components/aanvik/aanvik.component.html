The Aanvik TECfest 2.0

The Electronics club of VIT is back with The Aanvik TECfest 2.0 on 31.10.18 (Wednesday) from 6:00-7:30 at Greenos. We present to you a combo of :-

1) Project expo in upcoming domains of healthcare, smart cities, security, agriculture and a lot more working models with their live demonstration of compiling codes and running motors.


2) A Technical write up contest where you get a stage to present your innovative and self made ideas infront of the crowd.

The important instructions for the contest to be held are as follows:-

  • We have 5 interesting domains for you. Submit an abstract under any one of them.
  • You can register individually or with a partner (team of 2)
  • Enthusiasts from all years and departments can participate.
  • Make sure to keep your abstract within 200 words (The simpler, the better)
  • In addition to your abstract, you can also support your idea with additional flowcharts/infographics/diagrams etc.
  • The best ideas will be shortlisted for a final pitch on the day of Aanvik TECfest. where in the ideas based on the presentation skills will be judged by the esteemed professors of VIT.
  • Winners of the final round can take home gift hampers, exciting goodies, recognition and certificates from TEC.
  • Deadline for submitting the abstract: 11:59PM, 28th October 2018

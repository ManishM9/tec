ABOUT US

Our Motto:

TEC aims to inculcate practical knowledge to the students, provide them platform to change their ideas into innovations and develop their skills to compete with the present world of electronics.

What we are:

They say technology is a never-ending curve and you always should be ready for new surprises. At The Electronics Club (TEC) of VIT, we believe that knowledge is not just to attain, but also to impart. Our passion and our focus are primarily on electronics. Education is not just about reading big fat textbooks or memorizing a zillion formulae; that is what we call information. Education begins when we begin to experience the things that we study, and that is what TEC strives to provide. Through regular theoretical and Hands-on sessions, TEC supports students who are passionate about electronics and helps them in exploring new avenues of imagination. TEC is working on a number of projects, and is always open to more innovative and inspiring ideas. We believe that the success of any organization depends on the unity amongst the people in it. TEC is a well-knit group of 81 members and through regular group discussions and meetings, the smooth working of the club is ensured. Our intense work is well complemented with some fun. This is done by organizing a few trips and parties round the year. Our motto of helping others learn is further strengthened as the senior’s act as mentors for the juniors. In addition to this, several sessions are also kept for the junior members of the club that enable them further to achieve their dreams. The Electronics Club of VIT does not tell you what your goals should be, but surely helps you to move closer to them.

PROJECTS

Gesture And Pupil Controlled Wheelchair

Technological innovations in the field of medicine and healthcare is gaining momentum every day. As innovations are getting more creative, the major aim of this project is to develop a wheel chair embedded with modern features to support the disabled people to move around better without much dependence on others. The prototype is to benefit the differently abled people with much easier operation and quicker response time and greater built in security. It has two supporting features- gesture control(partial disability) and iris control(complete immobility) which are is a boon to the specially abled to move around with no external assistance required.

An Optimal Approach To Solve Learning Disorders

Who knew that a worldwide health related issue, dyslexia and learning disorders (improving the pronunciation and lets them spell it efficiently) can be made simpler by just a handy game. An app developed in the form of a game which addresses the problem of dyslexia and helps the victim with pronunciations, helps them in spell words, thus solving the disorders faced in learning.

T-Farmation

Farmers are on a constant hunt for technological advancements which can reduce human intervention and make way to an advanced method of agriculture. We are working on making this dream come true to design a smart agriculture system by incorporating smart irrigation system, crop prediction system, weather forecast API, nutrient detection, Crop health monitoring and efficient supply of raw materials. This sure is to benefit the farmers and bring about a major change.

Early Detection Of Rheumatic Heart Diseases Using Machine Learning

Rheumatic Heart Disease is damage to one or more heart valves that remains unnoticed in the earlier stages and becomes difficult to cure in its later stages. The project work on detecting abnormalities present in phonocardiogram or heart Sounds of an affected person from a healthy one. Using Kalman filtering technique for an adaptive filtration of the heart sound followed by feature extraction which will be used to train a convolution neural net. Rheumatic Heart Disease is a chronic, disabling and sometimes fatal but if identifies is 100% curable.

WE-SHARE

The dependency of our country on non-renewable sources of energy has been one of the main reasons for this problem. In this solution, we would be developing a smart grid infrastructure for the analysis and sharing of energy resources at home. The grid links all the household in a network and smartly switches the connection on user’s necessities. A person can invite other app users to join their network and transact energy. Different lenders can set their bid for multiple units of their excess energy and a borrower in need can choose the most efficient bid for himself. The feature will ensure that no part of the city is left in darkness wherein the energy is going waste in another part as the concept says “we-share”.

Autonomous Bot

Automation has its start with the building of bots. Autonomous navigation which makes possible a number of common tasks like self and fast delivery or tread along unsafe paths to acquire and bring back essential information is always a dire need. The main aim of this project is to create a bot which can travel itself anywhere using shortest of all possible paths. Bot needs to train a map once to identify various possible paths. The bot can travel both inside and outside the buildings. This finds effective application in various industries and can have a great impact to the defence and homeland security department.

Gesture Controlled Air Guitar

Playing guitar is a skill which is acquired by practice. The aim of this project is to allow everyone to create music. We have created a gesture-controlled air guitar in which the music will be played using bands worn in each hand. The bands will be used to select the chord/note and time to be played. The band's will connect and send the data to a computer via Wi-Fi locally and sound would be generated using MATLAB.

Swarm Robotics

Swarm robotics is an approach to the coordination of multiple robots as a system which consist of large numbers of mostly simple physical robots. It is supposed that a desired collective behavior emerges from the interactions between the robots and interactions of robots with the environment. All these small robots work in coordination to achieve a same purpose.

EMBED

Everything started with an Australian inventor by the name of Paul Eisler, who made a radio using the printed circuit board. You might not have considered just how ubiquitous printed circuit boards are. Starting from the alarm clock that starts your day, microwave that pops up a toasted bread to sending the Apollo 11 astronauts to the moon and back home again safely, PCB’s have always played the protagonist of the story. You’re likely reading this from a device that contains a PCB! They’ve become a really integral aspect of modern tech and are evolving every minute. Find out everything about PCB from the 4th edition of the workshop by TEC-VIT. A complete hands-on workshop to be conducted in collaboration with Enthu Technology Solutions India where the participants can take the kits back home with knowledge enhanced and interest intensified to explore more. Noteworthy concepts, designing on EAGLE, soldering, etching, imprinting, PCB prototyping, and the list goes on! Find yourself in the lost world of printed circuit boards at EMBED 4.0
